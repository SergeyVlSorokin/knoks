# Define MVP reporting scope

Type: grilling
Status: resolved

Part of: [Specify a consulting time-entry MVP](../map.md)

## Question

Does the MVP need any reporting surface beyond Members' weekly-grid summaries, Administrators' Available Billable Time review, and each Client's Invoice Bases; if so, which actor's concrete operational question must it answer and what is the minimum information needed to answer it?

## Answer

The MVP adds no standalone reporting surface. Its existing operational views answer the in-scope questions: Members see Billable, Non-billable, and Total duration in the weekly grid; Administrators inspect Available Billable Time with the already-decided contextual Non-billable totals and outside-period backlog; and each Client's immutable Invoice Bases provide the historical record of time prepared for external invoicing.

The MVP therefore has no separate dashboard, cross-Client or cross-Member summary, utilization or trend analysis, configurable report, or reporting-specific saved view. If a concrete recurring question later proves that these operational views are insufficient, that evidence should drive a separately scoped reporting addition rather than speculative aggregation in this MVP.
