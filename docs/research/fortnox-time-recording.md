# Fortnox time recording: official-source findings

**Purpose.** Citation-ready, source-limited findings for a comparison of time-recording products. This note makes no claims about any other product.

**Research method.** Only Fortnox-owned product pages and Fortnox Support pages were used. Support-page metadata identifies the publisher as Fortnox. This review was completed **2026-07-20**. “Not established” means the reviewed sources do not support the claim; it is not a claim that the capability is absent.

## Terminology and scope

Fortnox’s current product name in the sources below is **Fortnox Tid**; its support category is **Tid**. The reviewed sources do **not** call the product `Tidredovisning`. The public product page uses the phrase *tidrapportering* descriptively, not as the product name. Use **Fortnox Tid** when naming the product in reader-facing copy.

## Package and prerequisite distinctions

The current Fortnox package article distinguishes recommended packages for sole traders (Mini, Liten, Mellan, Stor) and companies with employees (Mini+, Mellan+, Stor+), while saying they are recommendations rather than hard eligibility rules. In its listed inclusions, it shows Fakturering from Liten/Mellan+ upward, Lön in Mellan+ and Stor+, and Rapport & Analys plus in Stor/Stor+.

This source describes Fortnox Tid in its programme overview but does **not** name Tid as included in any listed package. It therefore does not establish which package includes Tid or the price of Tid. Do not infer either point from this note. The public Tid product page independently says the product is an add-on ordered after choosing a starter package.

Source: [What is included in Fortnox’s packages?](https://support.fortnox.se/hantera-fortnox/abonnemang-och-avtal/vad-ingar-i-de-paket-fortnox-erbjuder) — **Fortnox packages**; published 2025-07-07; modified 2026-07-03; accessed 2026-07-20.

## Important non-claims

Do not use the reviewed sources to claim any of the following:

- GPS/location tracking, clock-in/out hardware, or customer approval.
- A complete time-entry approval workflow, approval roles, or approval history. “Attest i Tid” is mentioned in setup material, but its workflow is not established by these sources.
- That **Personalattest** or **Leverantörsfakturaattest** approves time entries. The package source identifies Personalattest as a digital-attestation add-on to Fortnox Lön and Leverantörsfakturaattest as supplier-invoice attestation; neither is equated with approval of Tid records.
- That Tid is included in a particular package, or a price for it.
- That `Tidredovisning` is Fortnox’s current product brand.
